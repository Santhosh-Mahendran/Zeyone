Return and Refund Policy

At Meta Ads live workshop, we strive to provide a valuable and enriching learning experience. Please read our return and refund policy carefully:

1. Eligibility for Refund

  • Refund requests must be submitted within the first 2 days of the workshop start date. After this period, no refunds will be issued.
  • Participants must provide a valid reason for the refund request, which will be evaluated on a case-by-case basis.

2. Refund Process

  • If your refund request is approved, the amount will be credited to your original payment method within 7–10 business days.

3. Non-Refundable Cases

  • Failure to attend the workshop without prior notice.
  • Dropping out of the workshop after the first 2 days.
  • Incomplete participation or dissatisfaction due to factors beyond our control (e.g., participant’s internet issues, missed sessions).

4. Workshop Cancellation

  • In the rare event that we cancel the workshop, participants will receive a full refund.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, please reach out to us at zeyonedigital@gmail.com.
