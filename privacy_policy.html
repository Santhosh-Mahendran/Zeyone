Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how zeyone.store collects, uses, and discloses your Personal Information when you visit or make a purchase from the Site.

Collecting Personal Information

When you visit the Site, we collect certain information about your device, your interaction with the Site, and information necessary to process your purchases. We may also collect additional information if you contact us for customer support. In this Privacy Policy, we refer to any information that can uniquely identify an individual as “Personal Information.”

Device Information

  • Examples: version of web browser, IP address, time zone, cookie information, sites or products viewed, search terms, and interactions.
  • Purpose: To optimize site performance and analytics.
  • Source: Automatically collected using cookies, log files, web beacons, tags, or pixels.

Order Information

  • Examples: Name, billing address, shipping address, payment information, email address, and phone number.
  • Purpose: To fulfill orders, process payments, communicate with you, and screen for fraud.
  • Source: Collected from you.

Customer Support Information

  • Purpose: To provide customer support.
  • Source: Collected from you.

Using Personal Information

We use your personal information to provide our services, including product offerings, processing payments, fulfilling orders, and providing updates.

Retention

Cookies

Cookies enhance your experience by remembering preferences and providing analytics. We use functional, performance, advertising, and social media cookies.

Do Not Track

Changes
